Itinerary

Details

Imagine you’re fabulously wealthy with two summer homes and friends who go by names like Cartwright and Kensington. You’ve entered the world of the Evening Star, where the good life seems to come naturally. Captain Marco, who has taken the helm of many rock royalty-owned vessels, will navigate his sailboat through the beautiful waters off Sausalito. All you and your crew need to worry about is having a tremendously good time.

Captain Marco will help you chart your own route to suit your interests. Depending on conditions, you might sail under the Golden Gate Bridge, along the San Francisco city front, past Alcatraz, around Angel Island and through Raccoon straights.
